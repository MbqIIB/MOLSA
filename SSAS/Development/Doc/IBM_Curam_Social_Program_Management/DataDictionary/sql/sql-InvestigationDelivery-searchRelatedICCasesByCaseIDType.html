Database SQL Operation: InvestigationDelivery.searchRelatedICCasesByCaseIDType

Operation Details
Description: Returns list of related active integrated cases for the given investigation case.
Sterotype: nsmulti

SQL 
SELECT 
DISTINCT 
CASEHEADER.CASEID , 
CASEHEADER.CASEREFERENCE , 
CONCERNROLE.CONCERNROLENAME , 
USERS.FULLNAME , 
CASEHEADER.STARTDATE,
(SELECT USERS.FULLNAME FROM CASEHEADER , ORGOBJECTLINK,USERS WHERE CASEHEADER.OWNERORGOBJECTLINKID= ORGOBJECTLINK.ORGOBJECTLINKID AND ORGOBJECTLINK.USERNAME=USERS.USERNAME AND CASEHEADER.CASEID =:caseID) AS source 
INTO 
:caseID,
:caseReference,
:primaryClient,
:caseWorker,
:startDate,
:source
FROM CASERELATIONSHIP , CASEHEADER, CONCERNROLE,USERS, ORGOBJECTLINK
WHERE CASEHEADER.CASEID= CASERELATIONSHIP.RELATEDCASEID 
AND CASEHEADER.OWNERORGOBJECTLINKID= ORGOBJECTLINK.ORGOBJECTLINKID 
AND ORGOBJECTLINK.USERNAME= USERS.USERNAME 
AND CONCERNROLE.CONCERNROLEID= CASEHEADER.CONCERNROLEID
AND CASEHEADER.STATUSCODE=:caseStatus
AND CASEHEADER.INTEGRATEDCASETYPE=:integratedCaseType
AND CASERELATIONSHIP.CASEID =:caseID
