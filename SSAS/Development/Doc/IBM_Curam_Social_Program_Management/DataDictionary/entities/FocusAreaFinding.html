Database Table: FocusAreaFinding

Table Description
Focus Area Finding : The FocusAreaFinding entity captures details relating to focus area findings on a case audit.

This entity captures information regarding whether a specific focus area has been satisfied or not.

Example

An audit coordinator assigns a case audit to an auditor for examination. There are two focus areas to be addressed on the case audit, 'Benefit Accuracy' and 'Timeliness'. The auditor adds findings for 'Benefit Accuracy' and sets the satisfied indicator to 'Yes'. The auditor adds findings for 'Timeliness' and sets the satisfied indicator to 'No'.
