Database Table: ServicePlanDelivery

Table Description
Service plan delivery: A service plan offered by an agency to a client who is a member of an integrated case.

This table represents the link between a service plan and a case.

Example

An administrator creates a Return to Work service plan for participant Linda Smith who is a member of an Assistance integrated case. A Sustainable Employment goal is selected for this service plan. Linda Smith is unable to pay for transport to get to work, therefore sub-goal Transportation of type Overcome a Barrier is added to the service plan.

A Mileage Reimbursement plan item is added to the service plan to achieve the service plan goal.
