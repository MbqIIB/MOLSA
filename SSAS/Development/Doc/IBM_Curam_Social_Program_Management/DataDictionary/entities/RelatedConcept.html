Database Table: RelatedConcept

Table Description
Related Concept
:
A concept that can be attached to taxonomy terms to provide different views in to the taxonomy. 

Related concepts function like mini-directories or sub-sets, allowing users to quickly identify a cluster of services identified with a particular target population or problem/need.

Example


Child Abuse is a related concept that can be attached to taxonomy terms from different parts of the taxonomy.
