Database Table: ActivityRecurrence

Table Description
Activity Recurrence: How frequently an activity recurs.

In addition to scheduling once-off activities, a user can schedule recurring activities, i.e. activities that recur over a period.

Example

User Ronan Rooney schedules an activity 'team meeting' to take place on the first Monday of every month with 10 recurrences.
