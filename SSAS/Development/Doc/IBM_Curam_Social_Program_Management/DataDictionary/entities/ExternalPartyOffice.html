Database Table: ExternalPartyOffice

Table Description
External Party Office: An office(s) or location where the external party carries out its services.

Example

The Sisters of Charity is a community based organization (CBO). They have two locations where they carry out their services, one is a shelter they provide to the homeless and another is a center for the elderly.
