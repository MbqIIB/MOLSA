Database Table: SpecialCaution

Table Description
SpecialCaution: When managing participant a user may need to document concerns or issues related to that participant that other users should be aware of. These Special Cautions or alerts can include immediate concerns regarding the participant to be aware of. The intent of capturing this type of information is to ensure the safety of the participant and the organization.

Example

These Special Cautions or alerts can include immediate concerns regarding the person for example concerns regarding suicidal ideation. Long-term issues to be aware of for example history of running away, tendency towards violence when confronted. The intent of capturing this type of information is to ensure the safety of the person and the organization.
