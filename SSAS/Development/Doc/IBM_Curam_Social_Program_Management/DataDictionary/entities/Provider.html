Database Table: Provider

Table Description
Provider: A person or company that provides services to an organization's clients on the organization's behalf.

A person or company that provides services to an organization's clients on the organization's behalf.

Example

John's Day Care is a provider who delivers Day Care Services on behalf of the organization.
