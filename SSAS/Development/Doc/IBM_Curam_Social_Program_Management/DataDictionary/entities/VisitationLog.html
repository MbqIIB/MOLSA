Database Table: VisitationLog

Table Description
Visitation Log : A visitation log captures a visit which occurred on a particular date, involving at least one visitor or client visited.

Visitation logs enable the user to capture the details of actual visits/interactions that occur for a visitation plan. The visitation log records entered on a visitation plan will ultimately be used to calculate the compliancy of visits between one or more visitors and each client on the plan. This information feeds into visitation evaluations as part of the plan review.

Example

Daniel Smith is a 18 month child who has been placed in a foster care home as it was deemed by the child welfare agency that he was not safe staying at home with his mother, Julie Smith.
The child welfare caseworker dealing with Daniel creates an outcome plan for the family. The caseworker creates a visitation plan for Daniel and Julie to maintain relationships. The visitation plan outlines that Linda must make two face-to-face visits per month. Julie completes bi-monthly face-face visits with Daniel according to the visitation plan. The caseworker records these visits for the visitation plan.
