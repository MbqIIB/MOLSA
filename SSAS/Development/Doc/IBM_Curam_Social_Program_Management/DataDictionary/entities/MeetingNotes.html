Database Table: MeetingNotes

Table Description
Meeting Notes: The notes made at a meeting and recorded as part of meeting minutes.

Notes may be captured as part of minutes recorded for a meeting that occurred. Other details such as agenda, attendees, decisions, meeting actions may also be captured as part of the meeting minutes.

Example

John James is a teacher and is also a member of the MDT assigned to a Child Welfare social enterprise folder for Robert Smith and Linda Smith. John James is Robert's teacher and has noticed that Robert is frequently absent from school and decides to schedule a meeting with all the MDT members and Linda Smith to determine the reasons for Robert's absence from school and discuss a course of action to reduce this absenteeism. Once the meeting is held, John James records the minutes. As part of the meeting minutes, the following notes are recorded:

  • Linda Smith explained that Robert was absent from school because she needed help around the house as she was in fact ill and not Robert.
  • David Lane, the MDT Leader, explained that this is not acceptable and offered carer services as an alternate.
  • David Lane, the MDT Leader, requested that Linda Smith provide medical certifications as proof of illness in the future.
