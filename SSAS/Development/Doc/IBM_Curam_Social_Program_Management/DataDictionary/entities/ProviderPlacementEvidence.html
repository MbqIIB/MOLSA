Database Table: ProviderPlacementEvidence

Table Description
Provider Placement Evidence: The evidence for the provider placement.

The placements are used as evidence for generating placement payments. This contains the evidence information for the provider placements.

Example

For example, John Smith is a 15 year old boy who is to be placed in Lake County Detention Center for drug abuse starting from 15/02/2007 for six months. Cell 4 in Building A is allocated to John starting from 15/02/2007 till 14/08/2007. Information about the evidence required to generate payment for Lake County Detention Center is maintained here.
