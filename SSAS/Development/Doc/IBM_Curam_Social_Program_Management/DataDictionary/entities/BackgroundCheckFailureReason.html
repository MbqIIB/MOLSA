Database Table: BackgroundCheckFailureReason

Table Description
Background Check Failure Reason: A reason why a background check has failed.

If a background check fails, the reason is recorded so as to why a background check has failed can be maintained.

Example

ShopliftingTrespassing, Misdemeanor, Conviction, Felony, Conviction, Breaking and Entering, Hit and Run, Substantiated A/N Perpetrator, Inconclusive A/N Perpetrator
