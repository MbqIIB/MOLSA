Database Table: HearingServiceSupplier

Table Description
Hearing Service Supplier: A service supplier that provides a service at a hearing.

Hearing service supplier helps in an appeal hearing hearing like hearing translations between the attendees and eye examination of the appellants.

Example

An interpreter is an example of a hearing service supplier. An interpreter is a language translator who facilitates communication between attendees at a hearing.
