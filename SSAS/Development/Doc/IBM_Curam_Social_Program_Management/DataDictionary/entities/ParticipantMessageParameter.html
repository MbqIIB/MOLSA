Database Table: ParticipantMessageParameter

Table Description
Participant Message Parameter : A link between the ParticipantMessage and Parameter tables. This links a ParticipantMessage to a parameter that is to form part of the message. Parameters are referenced in associated properties, and their values are substituted into the messages when they are to be displayed to the participant.

Example

James Smith logs into the citizen account and sees message on the citizen account home page indicating 'Your latest payment of $140 was due on 2010-06-14. Click here to view the payment details. Your next payment is due on 2010-12-12. Click My Payments to view your payment history.' This entity is used to store the mapping between the name of the placeholder in the message property (${Payment.Amount}) and the actual value that is to be displayed in the message.
