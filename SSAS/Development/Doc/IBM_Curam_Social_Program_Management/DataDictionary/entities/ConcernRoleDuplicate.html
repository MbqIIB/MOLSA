Database Table: ConcernRoleDuplicate

Table Description
Concern Role Duplicate: A list of clients that have been identified as duplicates of one another.

For various reasons it sometimes happens that the organization records information about the same person or organization more than once. When the organization becomes aware that they have registered a person or a prospect person more than once, they need to merge the information recorded so that duplicate or conflicting information isn't used.

Example

James Smith is registered as a Person and Jimmy Smith as a Prospect in the system. If the user detects that these are the same individual, Jimmy Smith can be identified as a duplicate of James Smith.
