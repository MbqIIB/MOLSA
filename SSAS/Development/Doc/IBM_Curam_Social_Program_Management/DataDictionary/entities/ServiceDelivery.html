Database Table: ServiceDelivery

Table Description
Service Delivery : The delivery of a service to a client.

A service can be added to any type of case and outcome plans. Most of the fields on this table are optional as the details may be held elsewhere, for instance if the service is being delivered through the use of a product delivery, most of the delivery information will reside on the product delivery table. The deliveryTypeRelatedID can be used to link the service delivery to another delivery mechanism such as a product delivery. However, each service delivery must have a record on this table so that it can be linked correctly to outcome plans and cases.
For standard services delivered by third party providers( which do not require any rules/eligibility processing) the delivery information will reside solely on this table.

Example

James Smith was assessed for substance abuse issues and as a result the case worker scheduled 10 sessions of counseling from 01/01/2011 to 02/15/2011 provided by Dr. John Kelly. This entity stores the details of this service.

Technical Notes

Most of the fields on this entity are optional as the information may be held elsewhere, including on an external system. The deliveryTypeRelatedID can be used to link to additional delivery information held elsewhere. For each different deliveryType an implementation must be provided which retrieves all the service delivery information so it can be displayed on a plan. Out of the box Cúram supplies two delivery types, standard service deliveries provided by third party providers and product rules and evidence based service deliveries which allow payments to be made to a client in respect of the service.
