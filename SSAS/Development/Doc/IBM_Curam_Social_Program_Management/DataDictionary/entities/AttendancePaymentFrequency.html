Database Table: AttendancePaymentFrequency

Table Description
Attendance Payment Frequency: The default configuration for attendance tracking payment-related frequencies, applicable for all service offerings and providers paid based on attendance roster information.

The Attendance Payment Frequency is used to calculate the date on which the payment will be issued to the provider by the social organization. The frequency is applicable for all the service offering and provider in the organization.

Example

The organization sets the Attendance Payment Frequency as weekly with an effective date of the current date. The attendance-based payments will be generated for all the providers and services weekly effective from current date.
