Database Table: AdminLegalStatus

Table Description
AdminLegalStatus: A legal status refers to the concept of individuals having a particular place in society, relative to the law, as it determines the laws which affect them

A legal status refers to the concept of individuals having a particular place in society, relative to the law, as it determines the laws which affect them. Various terms are used by different jurisdictions to refer to people and their status under the law. Temporary Custody, Release of Custody, Crown Ward, Permanent Custody, Pre-adjudicated etc are some of the types of legal status configured.

Example

Legal status of type Temporary Custody, configured by admin, is used by the case worker to associate the legal status to case participant if he/she is in the temporary custody of the organization
