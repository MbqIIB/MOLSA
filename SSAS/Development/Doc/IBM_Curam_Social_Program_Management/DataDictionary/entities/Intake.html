Database Table: Intake

Table Description
Intake : Stores the information required for the Intake. Intake refers to the process of applying and establishing a claim for the products and/or services provided by an SEM agency.The intake process in most SEM agencies typically consists of some or all of the following high level steps - Screening, Application, Determination, Budgeting, Assessment & Planning
