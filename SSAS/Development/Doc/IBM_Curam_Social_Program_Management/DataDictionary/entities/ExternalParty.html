Database Table: ExternalParty

Table Description
External Party: A person, company or charity who interacts with the agency by carrying out specific functions on behalf of the organization's clients or potential clients.

The external party entity captures the information for the external party who interacts with the agency.

Example

The Sisters of Charity is a community based organization (CBO). They provide services to the people in the community in which they are located. One of the services they offer is to submit an application for benefits on behalf of a client.
