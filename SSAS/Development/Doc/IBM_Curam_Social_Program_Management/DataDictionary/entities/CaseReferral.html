Database Table: CaseReferral

Table Description
Case Referral: A referral of a person to a service supplier for a service.

This table holds a type of case event. Events are displayed in an events calendar for viewing by the user.

Example

John Smith has applied for disability allowance. A medical examination is scheduled for John Smith to determine the extent of his disability.
