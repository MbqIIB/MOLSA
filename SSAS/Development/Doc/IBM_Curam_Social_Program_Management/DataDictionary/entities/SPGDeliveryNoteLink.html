Database Table: SPGDeliveryNoteLink

Table Description
Service Plan Group Delivery Note Link : This entity is used to persist the notes linked to service plan group delivery. This table represents the link between a service plan group and a note.

Example

A case worker creates an integrated case for participant Linda Smith who has recently been involved in a work related accident. Within the integrated service plan view, he then creates a Health Care service plan group, so that all service plans created for Linda with health care related goals can be grouped and managed together. He then creates a note documenting a summary of a discussion that he had with Linda about her health care issues on the Health Care service plan group.
