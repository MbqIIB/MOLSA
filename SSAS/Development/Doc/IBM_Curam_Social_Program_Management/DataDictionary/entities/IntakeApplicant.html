Database Table: IntakeApplicant

Table Description
IntakeApplicant: A record of an individual who is making an application for assistance.

An application for assistance may contain one or more applicants. Applications may be submitted to an agency by paper or online. The basic information of each application received by an agency is recorded in the system.
