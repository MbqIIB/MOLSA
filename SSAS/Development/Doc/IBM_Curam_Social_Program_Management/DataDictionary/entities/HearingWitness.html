Database Table: HearingWitness

Table Description
Hearing Witness: A person who testifies under oath at a hearing.

The witness may be voluntary or involuntary.

Example

A witness is called to give evidence at a hearing on behalf of Linda Smith the appellant. The organization issues a summons to the witness as the witness does not volunteer to testify on behalf of the appellant.
