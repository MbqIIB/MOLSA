Database Table: License

Table Description
License: A permit that a provider may be required to have in order to operate or to deliver a certain service.

A permit that a provider may be required to have in order to operate or to deliver a certain service. A license may or may not be issued by the organization itself.

Example

A provider of foster care services may be required to have a 'State Foster Care License'.
