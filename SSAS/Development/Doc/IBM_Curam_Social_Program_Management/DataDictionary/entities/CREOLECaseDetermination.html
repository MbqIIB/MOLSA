Database Table: CREOLECaseDetermination

Table Description
CREOLE Case Determination : The determination of the varying eligibility, entitlement and explanation of a case for a CREOLE-enabled product.

For CREOLE-enabled cases, a Determination will give rise to a number of case decisions, where each decision represents a date on which the overall case eligibility and/or obtained objectives differs from the previous decision in the determination.

Example

A Delivery product delivery case is created for claimant Linda Smith.

User Ronan Rooney enters evidence for Linda's circumstances and chooses to view the pre-release eligibility results for the case. The system records a determination with a initReasonCode of 'Result of Pre-Release Case Adjudication', storing a number of case decisions (predicting some variation in Linda's eligibility and entitlement).

User Ronan Rooney activates the evidence and approves the case. The system records a determination with a initReasonCode of 'Result of Case Approval', also storing its case decisions. Ronan then actives the case and the system records a determination with a initReasonCode of 'Result of Case Adjudication' and stores its case decisions.

One month later, Linda reports a change in her circumstances and User Ronan Rooney enters and activate the evidence. The system retrieves the earlier determination and modifies its statusCode from 'Active' to 'Superseded', and stores a new determination (and case decisions) with a statusCode of 'Active'.
