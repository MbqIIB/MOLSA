Database Table: CaseParticipantRole

Table Description
Case Participant Role: A participant associated with a case.

A participant can have multiple case participant roles in a single case.

A participant can have many different roles in different cases.

A case may have one or more case participant roles.

Example

John Smith has three case participant roles:

  • for case 1:
    • John Smith is the 'Primary Client'; and
    • John Smith is also a 'Nominee'; and
  • for case 2 John Smith is a 'Correspondent'.
