Database Table: AuthenticationFactor

Table Description
Authentication Factor : Stores the configured authentication factors for the Citizen Self Service. Authentication factors are only requested when a Citizen Self Service account has been associated with a person on an external system( a person in Cúram). Authentication factors are administered in the Citizen Self Service application and are used to authenticate a person when they log into the Citizen Self Service, for example, date of birth, SSN. The authentication factors are verified by an external system.

Example

James Smith logs into the Citizen Self Service. He is asked to enter his user name and password. Once specified and validated, he is asked to enter his SSN and date of birth before he can successfully log in to the citizen account. This entity is used to store the administration details of the authentication factors SSN and date of birth.
