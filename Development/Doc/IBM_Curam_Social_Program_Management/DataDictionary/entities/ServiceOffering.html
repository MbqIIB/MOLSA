Database Table: ServiceOffering

Table Description
Service Offering: An offering from a Provider that a client can be authorized by the organization to receive.

An offering from a Provider that a client can be authorized by the organization to receive.

Example

Examples of a service offering are Special Needs Foster Care, Residential Care or Secure Housing.
