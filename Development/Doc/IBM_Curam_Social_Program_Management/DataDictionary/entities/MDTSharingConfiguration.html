Database Table: MDTSharingConfiguration

Table Description
Multidisciplinary Team Sharing Configuration : Holds the configured sharing details of a multidisciplinary team member or multidisciplinary team member role. Defines the sharing configurations for a team member or team member role for a various type of information.

Example

A teacher role is created, and a sharing is configured to be preselected for attachments. The result is a multidisciplinary team sharing record is created for the team member role storing the configured role sharing information for attachments.
