Database Table: TypicalPictureVersion

Table Description
DATypical Picture Version: A Typical Picture Version gives the typical answers to a Questionnaire for a specific Determination Outcome or that apply in certain circumstances.

A Typical Picture version gives the Typical answers to a questionnaire for a specific Determination Outcome or answers that apply in specific circumstances. A Typical Picture Version can be viewed, modified and deleted.
