Database Table: ConcernRoleContact

Table Description
Concern Role Contact : A party nominated by a participant to represent that participant in their interactions with the organization.

When a registered participant was not selected as the contact,the contact will be automatically registered as a participant of type representative.

Example

  • John Smith is a contact for his incapacitated brother.
  • ACME Ltd has two nominated contacts who manage all interactions between ACME Ltd and the organization.
