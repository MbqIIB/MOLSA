Database Table: ServiceGroup

Table Description
Service Group: A logical grouping of similar services.

A logical grouping of similar services, e.g. all foster care services (Traditional Foster Care, Special Needs Foster Care) may be in a group called Foster Care Services. This may be used to help a user to locate a particular type of service.

Example

Foster Care Services is a service group for all type of foster care services.
