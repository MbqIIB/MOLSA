Database Table: DeliveryMethod

Table Description
Delivery Method: A method by which financial transactions can be issued or received for a product.

For each product in Curam, one or more delivery methods is defined.

For each delivery method an offset can be defined. Delivery method offsets refer to the number of days prior to the due date which a Financial Component needs to be processed to ensure that the payment reaches the participant on time.

Example

The organization delivers two products:

  • for Product A, valid delivery methods are:
    • cash; and
    • check; and
  • for Product B, valid delivery methods are:
    • EFT; and
    • voucher.

For the Product A's check delivery method, the organization sets an offset of three days in advance to allow for bank clearing. This offset ensures that funds are available to the participant on the due date.
