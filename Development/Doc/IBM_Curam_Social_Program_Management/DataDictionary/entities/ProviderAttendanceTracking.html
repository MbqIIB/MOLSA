Database Table: ProviderAttendanceTracking

Table Description
Provider Attendance Tracking: The configuration settings related to attendance tracking at the provider level.

Attendance tracking configuration options which may vary from one provider to another.

Example

Carry's Child Care is a registered provider, and authorized to provide child care services to the clients of the organization. The organization sets the 'Paper Roster Required' indicator to true for this provider. When the roster is generated for the provider, a paper roster is also generated.
