Database Table: AppealReopenHistory

Table Description
Appeal Re-open History: The reopening of an appeal case.

A closed appeal case can be reopened if the attendance for a hearing is insufficient. The hearing attendance is considered insufficient when the appellant or respondent is not present at the hearing.

Example

Linda Smith applies for sickness benefit with an organization. It is calculated that Linda is entitled to $40 a week. Linda is dissatisfied with this rate of payment and submits a request for appeal to the organization. An appeal case is created on the system.

A hearing is scheduled. Linda, the appellant does not attend the hearing due to a family emergency. The appeal case is decided and closed. Linda subsequently requests that the appeal case be reopened.
