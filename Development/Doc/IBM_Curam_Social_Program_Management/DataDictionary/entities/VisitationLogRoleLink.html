Database Table: VisitationLogRoleLink

Table Description
Visitation Log Role Link : A link record which links a visitation role on a visitation plan with a visitation log.

Visitation roles live on the visitation plan and where particular roles are added to the visitation log, the equivalent roles from the plan are linked to the log via this record.

Example

Daniel Smith is a 18 month child who has been placed in a foster care home as it was deemed by the child welfare agency that he was not safe staying at home with his mother, Julie Smith. The child welfare caseworker dealing with Daniel creates an outcome plan for the family. The caseworker creates a visitation plan for Daniel and Julie to maintain relationships. Julie completes a visit with Daniel and the caseworker makes a record of this visit. A visitation log role link record is created to link Julie?s visitor record on the plan with the visitation log.
