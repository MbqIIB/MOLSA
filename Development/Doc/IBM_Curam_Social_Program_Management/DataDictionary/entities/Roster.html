Database Table: Roster

Table Description
Roster: Information about an Attendance Roster for a Concern role.

Concern roles such as provider, employer etc can track the attendance for their clients, employees through a roster. Each client is added as a roster line item to an attendance roster.

Example

An agency may generate a roster for one of its provider, Carrys Child Care, in order to track the attendance of its two clients, James Smith and Linda Smith. The roster will have two roster line items- one for James and one for Linda.
