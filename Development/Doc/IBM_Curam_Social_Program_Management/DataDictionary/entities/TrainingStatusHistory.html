Database Table: TrainingStatusHistory

Table Description
Training Status History: The status history of a provider or provider group member, or unassigned provider member training program.

The status of a provider or provider group member, or unassigned provider member training will change through its lifecycle.

Example

For example, the status for is set to 'Open' upon initial creation, then 'Approved' after being approved and then changed to 'Complete' once the provider/provider group member has completed the training.
