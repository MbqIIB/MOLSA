Database Table: BankBranch

Table Description
Bank Branch: An outlet of a bank.

A facility that performs some banking functions and is separate from a main office. A bank branch is typically identified through a Sort Code/Routing Number.

Example

John Smith banks at the 5th Avenue branch of Citibank in NY.
