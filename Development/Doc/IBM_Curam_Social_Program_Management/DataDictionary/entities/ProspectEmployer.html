Database Table: ProspectEmployer

Table Description
Prospect Employer: An employer who has either supplied insufficient information to be registered as an employer participant, or alternatively, the organization does not wish to register the prospect employer as an employer participant in their system.

Example

ACME Industries employs 100 permanent staff and 30 casual staff. ACME industries trades under the name 'Global Engineering' and operates in the 'construction' industry sector.
