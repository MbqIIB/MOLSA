Database Table: ClientInteraction

Table Description
Client Interaction : A summary view of communications and financial transaction types between the organization and a participant.

Phone calls made/received, letters sent/received and payments are examples of client interaction types.

Example

A view of client interactions for participant John Smith displays that a letter was sent to John on 1/1/2004 and a payment of $50 was made to him on 2/1/2004.
