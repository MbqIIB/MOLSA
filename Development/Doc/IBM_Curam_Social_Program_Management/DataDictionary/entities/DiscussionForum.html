Database Table: DiscussionForum

Table Description
Discussion Forum: A grouping of discussions.

A discussion forum can be associated with any type of entity, for example, a case or an incident. A forum can contain one or more discussions.

Example

Linda Smith, James Smith and their three children Robert, Lisa and Susan are added to a Child Welfare social enterprise folder so that they can be managed together as a family. Incidents of Child Neglect have been recorded for Robert, Lisa and Susan. A discussion is automatically created on incident creation. A predefined multidisciplinary team is assigned to the social enterprise folder to allow them to assess and manage the families needs. This team consists of a police officer, a doctor and child services agency worker.

This entity stores the link between the incident and the discussion created as a result of incident creation.
