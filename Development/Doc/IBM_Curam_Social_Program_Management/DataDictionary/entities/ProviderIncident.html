Database Table: ProviderIncident

Table Description
Provider Incident: Details information specifically related to the maintenance of Provider incidents for example, Whether an incident occurred at the provider facility.

Provider Incidents can be used to record and manage events that have or could have a direct or indirect negative effect on a provider related to the organization.

Example

An incident of assault has been reported between two staff members of Sally's Day Care who used abusive language against each other in front of the children. This entity stores details of the incident against Sally's Day Care
