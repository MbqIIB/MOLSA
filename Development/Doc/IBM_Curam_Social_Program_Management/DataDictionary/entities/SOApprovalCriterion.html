Database Table: SOApprovalCriterion

Table Description
Service Offering Approval Criterion: Service offering approval criteria which may be used to approve a provider to deliver a certain service offering.

Service offering approval criteria which may be used to approve a provider to deliver a certain service offering.

Example

The organization may request a home study and a background check to be completed before a provider can be approved to deliver the service offering traditional foster care.
