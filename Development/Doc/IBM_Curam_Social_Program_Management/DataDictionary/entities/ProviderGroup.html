Database Table: ProviderGroup

Table Description
Provider Group: An organization to which a cluster of providers are related.

An organization to which a cluster of providers are related.

Example

Catholic Charities is an umbrella organization which encompasses many providers who deliver many different services such as counseling, day care and foster care.
