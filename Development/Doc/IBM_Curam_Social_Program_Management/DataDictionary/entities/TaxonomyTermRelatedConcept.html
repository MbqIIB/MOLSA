Database Table: TaxonomyTermRelatedConcept

Table Description

Taxonomy Term Related Concept
: This link entity facilitates the association of Related Concept with the Taxonomy Term.

br /> The taxonomy term can have many related concepts and in turn a related concept can be associated with many taxonomy terms.
