Database Table: MDTMemberRole

Table Description
Multidisciplinary Team Member Role : Holds the details of a role that can be applied to a multidisciplinary team member. A multidisciplinary team member role defines the role that can be applied to a multidisciplinary team member and a brief description of the role. The levels of sharing for the various pieces of information are also defined for a role.

Example

A teacher role is created, and no sharing is specified for all pieces of information. The result is the agency worker can never share notes with members created for a teacher role.
