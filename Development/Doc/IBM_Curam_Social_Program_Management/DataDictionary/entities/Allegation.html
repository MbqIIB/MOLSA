Database Table: Allegation

Table Description
Allegation: Details of an allegation.

Details of an allegation.

Example

For example, Mary Smith alleges that John Smith has committed sexual abuse on his daughter Linda Smith. The abuse has taken place in the home on 16 June 2006.
