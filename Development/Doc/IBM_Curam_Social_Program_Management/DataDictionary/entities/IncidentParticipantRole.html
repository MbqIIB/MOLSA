Database Table: IncidentParticipantRole

Table Description
Incident Participant Role: A participant or user associated to an Incident and their respective role in the incident e.g. witness, perpetrator etc.

This entity allows for the associations between an Incident and the Curam Participants and/or Users who have a role in the incident.

Example

When an Incident such as a suspected abuse Incident is recorded, the suspect abuser, witnesses etc have roles in the incident.
