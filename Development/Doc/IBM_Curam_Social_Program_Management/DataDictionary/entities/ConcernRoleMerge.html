Database Table: ConcernRoleMerge

Table Description
Concern Role Merge: The key information about a merge.

Merging participant records involves marking one participant as a duplicate, and then manually choosing which details from the duplicate client to combine with those of the client that they have been marked a duplicate of.

Example

Jimmy Smith has been marked as a duplicate of James Smith. The user selects to merge selected details about Jimmy Smith (the duplicate) to James Smith (the master record).
