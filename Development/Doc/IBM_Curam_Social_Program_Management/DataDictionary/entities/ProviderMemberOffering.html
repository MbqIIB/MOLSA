Database Table: ProviderMemberOffering

Table Description
Provider Member Offering: A provider offering which a provider member delivers to the client who is authorized to receive the service.

The Provider Member Offering defines the period during which a provider Member is involved in delivering a service with a Provider to the organization's clients.

Example

For Example: Carry's Child Care is providing the service Child Infant Care Full Time to the Organization's clients. Carry Smith is a Provider Member of Carry's Child Care. Carry Smith is involved in delivering Child Infant Care Full Time service to Organization's client.
