Database Table: VerificationRequirement

Table Description
Verification Requirement: Defines rules governing the verification requirement for a piece of data.

Example

A piece of data, e.g. date of birth, may have different verification requirements depending on the case for which the data has been captured or used. For example, a date of birth captured at the integrated case level may have a particular verification requirement for the integrated case, however it may also have different verification requirements for the product delivery cases that have been added to the integrated case.

For instance, for the integrated case a copy of the birth certificate may be sufficient to satisfy the verification requirement but for a specific product added to the integrated case the original copy of the birth certificate may be required. Any document provided for a specific verification requirement must be applied to all verification requirements in respect of the particular item of data.

In the case of this example, an original copy of a birth certificate provided for the product delivery verification requirement would result in the date of birth also being verified for the integrated case.
