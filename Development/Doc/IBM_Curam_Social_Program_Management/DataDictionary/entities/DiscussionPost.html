Database Table: DiscussionPost

Table Description
Discussion Post: A comment added by a user to a discussion.

A discussion can contain one or more discussion posts.

Example

Linda Smith, James Smith and their three children Robert, Lisa and Susan are added to a Child Welfare social enterprise folder so that they can be managed together as a family. Incidents of Child Neglect have been recorded for Robert, Lisa and Susan. A discussion is automatically created on incident creation. A predefined multidisciplinary team is assigned to the social enterprise folder to allow them to assess and manage the families needs. This team consists of a police officer, a doctor and child services agency worker. The doctor posts a comment on the discussion created as a result of Lisa's incident to indicate that she should should be sent for a full health check.

This entity stores the comment posted by the doctor.
