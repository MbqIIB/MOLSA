Database Table: ScreeningType

Table Description
ScreeningType: This entity represents a screening. Screening is used to determine a citizen's potential eligibility to receive programs from a social agency. Screening can consist of two processes; a filtered screening that can be used to narrow down the programs that are undertaken for full eligibility screening.
