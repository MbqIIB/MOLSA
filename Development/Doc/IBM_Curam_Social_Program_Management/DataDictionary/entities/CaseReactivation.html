Database Table: CaseReactivation

Table Description
Case Reactivation: The re-opening of a product delivery case.

A user may wish to reactivate a product delivery (with a status of either 'Closed' or 'Pending Closure') following a notified change in a client's circumstances.

This table holds a type of case event. Events are displayed in an events calendar for viewing by the user.

Example

John Smith lost his job and was in receipt of a program called 'unemployment assistance'. John subsequently found a new job and his case was closed.

One month later John was made redundant/retrenched. His original case was reactivated as John was again entitled to receive 'unemployment assistance'.
