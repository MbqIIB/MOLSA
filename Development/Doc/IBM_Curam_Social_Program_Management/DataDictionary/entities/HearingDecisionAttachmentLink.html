Database Table: HearingDecisionAttachmentLink

Table Description
Hearing Decision Attachment Link: An attachment to an appeal case decision.

Decision attachments can be either external documents or documents created internally in the system.

Example

Linda Smith applies for sickness benefit with an organization. It is calculated that Linda is entitled to $40 a week. Linda is dissatisfied with this rate of payment and submits a request for appeal to the organization. A user creates an appeal case in the system. A hearing is scheduled. When the hearing is completed, a decision is made by a hearing official on Linda's case. The hearing official creates a decision document for the case outside the system and attaches the decision document to the decision in the system.
