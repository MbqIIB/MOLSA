Database Table: Product

Table Description
Product: An offering provided by the organization to participants.

A product may originate from an external product provider, which the organization administers on their behalf and offers it to participants as if it were an organization-owned product.

Example

Midway Social Services is a Human Services organization which has implemented Curam. Midway Social Services offers a 'sickness benefit' product.
