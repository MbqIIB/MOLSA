Database Table: TrainingServiceOffering

Table Description
Training Service Offering: Configuration details for training service offering.

Additional criteria that are specific to the management of a training service offering.

Example

For example, an agency may identify that Separation, Placement, and Reunification in Family-Centered Child Protective Services training requires approval to authorize a provider member to receive this training service. The agency further confirms that the provider member needs to undergo 10 units of training before it can be marked as completed.
