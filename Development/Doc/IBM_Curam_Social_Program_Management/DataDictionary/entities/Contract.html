Database Table: Contract

Table Description
Contract: A formal agreement between the organization and a service supplier or product provider.

The contract relates to the provision of a product/service by the product provider or service supplier to the organization.

Example

Dr. Jim Smith is a service supplier. Dr Smith has a contract with the organization to provide medical examinations to assess the level of disability of veterans.
