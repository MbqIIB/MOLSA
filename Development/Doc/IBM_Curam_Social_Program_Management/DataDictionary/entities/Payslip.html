Database Table: Payslip

Table Description
Payslip : A payslip is a physical record of the instruction line item(s) that pertain to a particular payment or liability for a participant.Payslips may be issued to a participant, case nominee, or third party for record keeping purposes. The content for the three different kinds of payslips is similar and contains a header with recipient details, payment or liability details, and a section containing instruction line item details for each instruction line item in the payment or liability.
