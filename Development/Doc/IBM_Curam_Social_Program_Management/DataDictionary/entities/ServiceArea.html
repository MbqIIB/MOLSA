Database Table: ServiceArea

Table Description
ServiceArea: A service area is a region defined by the counties and zip codes that are added to the service area. The service area can then be associated with a local office for programs and PDF forms. This is used to inform a citizen of the appropriate local office based on the the county or zip code within which the citizen resides.
