Database Table: ServiceDeliveryConfiguration

Table Description
Service Delivery Configuration: The configurations used by the service delivery when providing the service associated with the configuration.

The service delivery configuration corresponds to a service offering. These configurations will be referred by the service delivery when a service offering is used for the service delivery.
