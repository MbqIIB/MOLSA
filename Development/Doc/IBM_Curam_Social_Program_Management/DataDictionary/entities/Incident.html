Database Table: Incident

Table Description
Incident: An event that has or could have a direct or indirect negative affect on a client of the agency.

Curam allows for the recording of incidents that have affected participants. Files may be attached to the incident and Participants may be added.

Example

Incidents of Child Neglect have been recorded for Robert, Lisa and Susan. This entity stores the details of the Child Neglect incidents raised for Robert, Lisa and Susan.
