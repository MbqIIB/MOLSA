Database Table: ProviderParty

Table Description
Provider Party: Provider party are the individuals associated to the provider that have a significant internal relationship to the provider.

Provider party are the individuals associated to the provider that have a significant internal relationship to the provider.

Example

For example, employees and family members associated with the provider who might be involved in providing services to the agency's clients.
