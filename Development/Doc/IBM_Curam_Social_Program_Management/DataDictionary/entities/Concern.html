Database Table: Concern

Table Description
Concern: A party that has an interaction with the organization, either directly or indirectly, about which the organization wishes to record information based on their interaction role.

A concern can have many concern role types. Concern role types for which Curam records information are Person, Prospect Person, Employer, Prospect Employer, Product Provider, Service Supplier, Information Provider, Representative, Utility, External Party.

In Curam the terms concern and participant are interchangeable.

Example

John Smith is in receipt of sickness benefit from the organization. John Smith is a concern of type person.
