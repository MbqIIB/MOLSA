Database Table: FederalAllowableComponent

Table Description
Federal Allowable Component : A component for which associated activities count towards a state's federal participation rate.

Federal allowable components can be associated with one of more activities (actions and services) and activities can be associated with one or more federal allowable components. If a client participates in any of these activities their hours will be counted under the federal allowable component. Programs can also be associated with a federal allowable component.
