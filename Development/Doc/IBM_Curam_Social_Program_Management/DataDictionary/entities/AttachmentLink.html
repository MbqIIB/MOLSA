Database Table: AttachmentLink

Table Description
Attachment Link: A relationship between an attachment and another object or record, such as an incident or discussion record.

This entity allows for the associations between attachments and other records such as incidents, discussions, meeting minutes.

Example

When an incident such as a suspected abuse incident is recorded for a Child Welfare social enterprise folder, medical reports from a doctor and statements from witnesses and the reporter may be captured and attached to the incident.
