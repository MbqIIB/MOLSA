Table Description
DM_FACTINVESTIGATION : Investigation Fact Table

Description:

An Investigation is a case type. An investigation case can be created at the integrated case level, at the product delivery case level or as a standalone investigation and is an inquiry into the circumstances surrounding an allegation.
