Database Table: S_CONTACTLOGATTENDEE

Table Description
S_CONTACTLOGATTENDEE : Staging table Contact Log Attendee

Description:

A record of the PARTICIPANTS in a LOG. These are the WORKERS and the PARTICIPANTS who were involved in the contact being recorded.
