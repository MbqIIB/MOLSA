Database Table: S_PRODUCT

Table Description
S_PRODUCT : Staging table Product

Description:

A product may originate from an external PROVIDER, which the ORGANIZATION administers on their behalf and offers it to PARTICIPANTS as if it were an ORGANIZATION-owned product.
