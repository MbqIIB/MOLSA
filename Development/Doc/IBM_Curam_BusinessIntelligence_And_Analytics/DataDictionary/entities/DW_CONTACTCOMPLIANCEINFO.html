Database Table: DW_CONTACTCOMPLIANCEINFO

Table Description
DW_CONTACTCOMPLIANCEINFO : Contact Compliance Information Central Warehouse Table

Description:

Cúram stores additional information (e.g. the next court date, last contact date) as related to a CLIENTS to inform the user of pertinent information in their dashboard pods.
